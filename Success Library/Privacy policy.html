Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information at Success Library.

We collect personal information such as your name, email, contact number, and batch details during registration. We also collect data related to your usage of the library website to enhance your experience.

Your personal data is used to manage your library membership, provide notifications about services, and improve our offerings. We do not sell or share your data with third parties without your consent.

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

4. Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand user behavior and optimize our website. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data stored with us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@successlibrary.com.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and significant updates will be communicated to you via email or notification.
